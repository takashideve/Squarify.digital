How Strabo Launched Their Startup with no-code

Strabo is a startup that took a unique approach to launch its financial tracking platform by using no-code tools.

Strabo helps users manage and view all their global financial accounts, investments, and assets from one place, making it a powerful tool for people with complex, international finances.

This would usually require a lot of complex coding and high-budget development, but the team at Strabo turned to a no-code solution to build their platform faster and more affordably.

Identifying a Market Need

The founders of Strabo saw a gap in financial management for people who live or work across borders.

Managing multiple bank accounts, tracking assets, and dealing with currency differences can be complex.

Strabo's mission was to simplify this by giving users a way to manage everything in one view.

But they needed to validate their idea quickly and avoid the costly, time-consuming process of hiring developers to code a financial app from scratch.

Leveraging No-Code with Bubble

To bring their vision to life, Strabo's team used Bubble, a no-code platform that enables anyone to build fully functioning web apps through a drag-and-drop interface.

Bubble provided all the core building blocks needed for a financial platform: user logins, databases, workflows, and responsive design.

This allowed Strabo to create a functioning app without writing any code, saving time and money in the early stages.

Using Bubble, Strabo was able to integrate important functionalities into their platform, like pulling real-time data from bank accounts, thanks to Bubble's API integrations.

This was crucial because Strabo's value to users depended on live financial data.

Bubble's powerful backend also enabled them to structure their app's data in ways that ensured user data was secure a key requirement for any financial tool.

Why No-Code Was the Right Fit

Using no-code brought many advantages to Strabo. Firstly, it kept their initial costs low. Typically, developing a financial platform would require a significant investment in custom coding and hiring developers.

But Bubble allowed Strabo's small team to build the entire platform themselves, which was both cost-effective and flexible.

Secondly, the no-code approach enabled Strabo to adapt quickly to feedback.

In a financial application, user experience is crucial, and Strabo's team could use Bubble's easy design tools to improve the interface based on user needs.

This made it possible for them to enhance the app without waiting for lengthy coding cycles.

With no-code, they could make fast changes, refine features, and get their platform ready for market quicker than traditional development would allow.

Focusing on Growth Over Complex Code

With the technical complexity handled by Bubble, Strabo's founders could focus on the parts of the business that mattered most like building relationships with financial partners, marketing, and user experience.

Instead of dedicating time to managing a complicated tech stack, they were able to prioritize customer acquisition and further enhance the features that users found valuable.

This approach was key to their growth, allowing them to refine the platform based on what early users wanted.

The Benefits of No-Code in Fintech

Strabo's success shows how no-code can empower startups in industries that require high functionality and user trust, like financial technology.

No-code platforms like Bubble are robust enough to support complex applications, so Strabo could create a secure, scalable solution without needing custom development right away.

As Strabo grows, they may choose to transition some parts to custom code, but the no-code foundation allowed them to start and gain traction at a fraction of the usual time and cost.

Strabo's story illustrates how no-code tools make launching a sophisticated product possible even for those without technical expertise.

This no-code approach is becoming a game-changer for entrepreneurs, making it feasible to test and build new ideas without a technical background.

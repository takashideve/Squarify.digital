Build your mobile app using native bubble

Introduction

Since Bubble's groundbreaking announcements at BubbleCon, the no-code landscape for mobile app development has transformed dramatically.

Bubble.io, traditionally seen as a web app powerhouse, has evolved into a robust solution for native mobile app development. Now, with Bubble's 2025-native capabilities, you can build fully functional, high-performing mobile apps without traditional coding.

Here's a guide on how to leverage Bubble's new native tools for creating a successful mobile app in 2025.

1. Designing with Bubble's components

Bubble now includes a comprehensive suite of native mobile components specifically optimized for mobile screens and touch interactions. Previously, Bubble's web-first approach required complex adjustments to deliver mobile experiences, but with new mobile-first options, you can use:

- Mobile-Specific Layouts: Create app screens that are optimized for touch, swipe, and scroll interactions.

- Gesture Controls: Add mobile gestures like swipe actions, long presses, and pinches, making your app feel native and intuitive.

- Enhanced UI Elements: Bubble's library now includes native-feeling elements such as bottom navigation bars, tab views, and floating action buttons to match typical mobile app UX.

2. Bubble's Mobile Plugins

At BubbleCon, Bubble introduced an expanded mobile plugin library, offering access to native device capabilities, such as:

- Camera and Location Access: Integrate real-time GPS data and camera features for apps that require geolocation or photography, like social or on-demand services.

- Push Notifications: Engage users with push notifications powered by Bubble's native notification API, allowing for real-time updates, reminders, and promotional messages.

- In-App Purchases and Payments: Seamlessly integrate payment options, including in-app purchases, making monetization easier and enabling subscription-based models directly in your app.

3. Bubble's Mobile Engine Performance

Bubble's mobile engine, unveiled at BubbleCon, improves app performance by optimizing code execution for mobile devices. To make your app feel fluid and responsive:

- Leverage Asynchronous Workflows: Reduce load times by setting up workflows that operate in the background, ensuring quick user interactions.

- Utilize Local Storage: Bubble now allows data caching locally on devices, so users experience faster loading times and offline functionality.

4. Publishing Directly to App Stores

A significant post-BubbleCon enhancement is Bubble's direct publishing tools, which allow creators to export and publish their apps on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Bubble simplifies the complex publishing process with:

- App Store Compliance Checks: Built-in guidance to meet app store guidelines for both iOS and Android, reducing rejections.

- Automated Builds: Bubble now generates the necessary build files, letting you submit your app directly to app stores with minimal configuration.

Conclusion

Thanks to Bubble's native-first developments, building a mobile app in 2025 is simpler and more powerful than ever before. From mobile-focused design elements to seamless app store publishing, Bubble empowers creators to develop high-quality native mobile apps without needing to code.

Dive into these features, start experimenting, and unlock the potential of Bubble to create standout mobile experiences for your users.

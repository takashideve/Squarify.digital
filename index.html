Bubble is a no-code platform that lets you build web apps without needing to know how to code. It's been around for a while, with millions of users and solid backing. With Bubble, you can design, build, and launch apps all in one place.
With Bubble, you can create almost any web app you can think of. You handle everything from design, data storage, logic, and even add third-party services like payment gateways or APIs. Basically, you can build full-fledged apps without writing code.
